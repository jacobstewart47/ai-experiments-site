Figma Make

I started on Figma Make, selecting the first the Web Suitcase library and then the general components. Neither worked as expected, so I had to manually adjust some styling to make it more on brand. I started with a basic prompt and after 20+ small iterations later, the prototype works OKAY-ish, except it crashes whenever I tried to explain the logic behind multicity.

Afterwards, I wanted to test the same prompt with Gemini, and even though the initial results were faster, it started to crash whenever I asked to change anything (even colors) but that might have been a bug as other people where experiencing the same. However it also crashes when I tried to add multicity, and editing the styling might be harder as I cannot link the Figma library.

What was the biggest challenge?

Neither of the models could understand the logic for multicity, they crashed. Figma Make didnt used the library I connected in an expected way, had to do some styling manually. Gemini crashed a lot with the minimal request.

What was the biggest learning?

Having a strong prompt from the beginning helps you get a better result, this includes explaining the logic of the different scenarios you are covering (a lot of if/then)
